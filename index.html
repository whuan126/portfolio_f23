Experience

        Brain Game Center

During my time at BGC, I had the privilege of being part of an incredible team that pushed the boundaries of virtual reality technology. Our focus was the development of a Spatial Release application, specifically designed for the Meta Quest 2 headset. Working directly under the guidance of two senior developers, I immersed myself in a world of cutting-edge software development practices. Their mentorship was invaluable as I learned to work in a team, implement feedback, and create insightful documentation.

My specific role was the creation of various input systems within the VR environment. These systems needed to be versatile and capable of accommodating over thirty different inputs seamlessly. This demanding task pushed the limits of my problem-solving abilities, resulting in the creation of six highly adaptable systems. Working alongside a dynamic team of interns, programmers, and researchers, we diligently documented the project's progress and collaboratively debugged code to ensure a high level of code quality and functionality. Additionally, we created playable demos that effectively showed the capabilities of our Spatial Release application, making it accessible and engaging for a wider audience.

                  iD Tech

As a programming instructor at iD Tech, I dedicated an entire summer to overseeing a multitude of projects spanning both Unity and Unreal Engine. In this dynamic and high-energy classroom setting, I was committed to improvement, both in the curriculum and the progress of each student. My key responsibility was acting as a bridge between parents, students, and fellow instructors, ensuring that every user story and learning goal was not just met, but exceeded. I took every challenge to heart and worked diligently to ensure that every participant left my program with a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction.

My motto, "I'm not sure, but let's find out together," instilled a sense of curiosity and determination in my students. I actively encouraged them to ask questions and explore solutions collaboratively, fostering a supportive learning environment. Additionally, I dedicated significant time to assisting my fellow instructors in debugging projects, providing inspiration, and offering moral support. This collaborative spirit ensured that we functioned like a well-oiled machine, delivering a seamless and enriching experience to all participants.
